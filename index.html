Yuri Gil Dantas

Who am I?

I am Yuri Gil Dantas, Brazilian, and old enough to hide my age. I am currently a research assistant at fortiss GmbH, Munich, Germany.

Academic and research career so far

I obtained my bachelor's and master's degree in Computer Science from Federal University of Paraiba, Brazil. Next, I moved to Germany to work as a research assistant at MAIS, TU Darmstadt. My research at MAIS focused on the development of runtime monitoring and enforcement mechanisms to mitigate cybersecurity attacks. I am currently a research assistant at fortiss GmbH, Munich, Germany. My current research at fortiss focuses on the development of techniques to integrate and automate safety and security methods.

Research interests

  • Denial of service attacks and defenses
  • Model-driven safety and security
  • Runtime monitoring and enforcement
  • Software-Defined Networking
